What is GROA?

It is a clinical decision support system to walk practitioners through the medical managment plan. It was initiated in 2016 to revolutionized the clinical practice toward abundance and stabilization. Its primary goal is to maximize the clinical outcomes.

Tailoring

Built to appreciate both the physiological and pathological parameters to dynamically evolves its recommendations, both macro- and micro.

Decomplicate

It cuts deeper into the complexity of diseases natural history and the practice guidelines to connect the dots and produce a reliable information.

Intiutive

The app flow, design, and information architecture are based on the clinical intuition and how real doctors mentally process the clinical decision to deliver the recommendations as fast as possible.
